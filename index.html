Who We Are

About Way To Go

Way To Go New Jersey is a locally owned and operated school bus company proudly serving Lakewood, NJ and surrounding communities. We understand that parents entrust us with their most precious cargo, and we take that responsibility seriously every single day.

Our team of professional drivers is fully licensed, insured, and background-checked. We maintain a modern, well-serviced fleet to ensure safe and comfortable rides for every student. From daily school routes to special charters, we deliver dependable transportation you can count on.

  • Locally Owned & Operated
  • Fully Licensed & Insured
  • Background-Checked Drivers
  • Modern, Well-Maintained Fleet
